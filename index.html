This site:

Welcome to Notes on Chemical Engineering for Graduate Students.

At this moment our team consists solely in Gustavo Kwak and Ricardo Wanderley, two Ph.D. students from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Our goal is to use this webpage as a repository for our class notes and, if possible, to turn it into a comprehensive database for Chemical Engineering matters. Hopefully, by the end of our Doctorate studies, we'll have built a good source for knowledge and education for Chemical Engineer graduates and undergraduates.

Please, feel free to contact us if you have any questions or suggestions regarding this webpage or its content and we'll try being as helpful as we can.
